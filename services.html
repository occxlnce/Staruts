Our Services

At Staruts Construction, we offer a range of specialized construction services to meet your needs. Whether it's concrete work, excavation, or road maintenance, our expert team ensures high-quality results. Explore our comprehensive solutions tailored to your project requirements.

Construction Consulting

We specialize in initiating construction plans, from the idealization stage to pre-development and final construction. Our services include visual impressions for investors, project feasibility research, and accurate supply estimation.

Construction Operations Signage

We provide durable signage solutions for construction sites to ensure clear visibility and access to important information during and after construction.

Roads Construction and Routine Maintenance

We manage efficient road construction projects and offer routine maintenance services such as road markings, resurfacing, and drainage systems to ensure smooth traffic flow and minimal disruptions.

Refurbishments and Renovations

We provide both structural and aesthetic refurbishments for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings, including reinforced concrete structures, dam lining, and excavation services.

Building Contracts for Residential and Industrial Sectors

Our team organizes construction teams and laborers to execute residential and industrial building projects, while also empowering workers to build their own legacy in the construction industry.

Reinforced Concrete and Structural Work

We offer designs and construction for concrete masonry buildings and steel structural work, including plastering, painting, and truss designs for both residential and industrial sectors.

Earthworks

We specialize in bulk earthworks and dam construction, including excavation, desludging, and lining of dams, ensuring efficient groundwork for various construction projects.

Pipe Laying and Excavations

We specialize in bulk earthworks and dam construction, including excavation, desludging, and lining of dams, ensuring efficient groundwork for various construction projects.

Transport and Plant Hire

We supply and operate heavy earthmoving machinery for mining, construction, and earthmoving industries. Our fleet is well-maintained and operated by skilled professionals to ensure efficient project delivery.

Telecommunications Services

Staruts Construction & Projects (Pty) Ltd offers a wide array of telecommunications services to support and maintain network infrastructure. We provide corrective and preventive maintenance, transmission fault resolution, and specialized services in wireless networks, fiber installation, and fixed line networks. Our highly skilled team ensures seamless communication across all platforms, enhancing network performance and reliability.

Our Telecommunications Services Include:

- Corrective Maintenance: Addressing network availability issues, vandalized sites, and ad-hoc duties like site investigations and generator refueling.
- Transmission Faults: Identifying and clearing RF and site alarms, sector failures, and VCWR alarms.
- Microwave Line Services: Providing EMS services for vandalized sites and handling microwave lines.
- Preventive Maintenance: Regular maintenance of sites, mobile/RF equipment, air conditioners, and electrical systems to prevent network failures.


Wireless Network Services

We specialize in RF, Line of Sight (LOS), and Technical Site Surveys (TSS) to optimize wireless network performance. Our installation and commissioning services for BTS, BSC, and MSC ensure proper setup and function of wireless infrastructure. Additionally, we handle optical fiber laying and testing, tower foundations, and drive test analysis for 2G, 3G, and LTE networks.



Fibre Installation & Fixed Line Networks


Our fibre installation services include:
- Fibre Testing: Ensuring the integrity of installed fiber networks.
- Duct Installation: Laying ducts for fiber cables, including trenching and fiber floating.
- Poles and Stay Installations: Aerial fiber hauling and installation.
- Termination and Testing: Presenting fibers on termination equipment and updating drawings as per site builds.

Fixed line network services include:
- New Voice and ADSL Installations: For high-quality fixed line services.
- Fault Maintenance: Repairing existing voice and ADSL services to ensure uninterrupted communication.
- Installation of voice and ADSL services: Efficient installation of new or upgraded services for residential or commercial needs.
